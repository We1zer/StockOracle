Our Prediction Models

LSTM Model

Our Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural network excels at capturing long-term dependencies in time series data, making it ideal for stock price prediction.

  • Pattern recognition in time series
  • 89% average accuracy rate
  • Excellent for medium-term forecasting
  • Processes 5 years of historical data

GRU Model

Our Gated Recurrent Unit (GRU) model provides efficient computation while maintaining high accuracy for short-term price predictions.

  • Faster training and execution
  • 87% average accuracy rate
  • Specialized for short-term forecasting
  • Advanced vanishing gradient handling

Transformer Model

Our state-of-the-art Transformer model utilizes attention mechanisms to identify complex relationships between market variables.

  • Self-attention mechanism
  • 92% peak accuracy rate
  • Multi-timeframe predictions
  • Integration of market sentiment data

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your stock predictions?

Our prediction models achieve varying levels of accuracy depending on the market conditions and timeframe. On average, our models maintain 85-92% accuracy for next-day predictions and 78-85% for 7-day forecasts. We transparently display each model's accuracy metrics so you can assess reliability yourself.

How often are predictions updated?

Our prediction models are updated daily after market close to incorporate the latest market data. Sentiment analysis is performed continuously throughout trading hours, with updates every 30 minutes.

Which stocks do you support?

We currently support over 500 stocks, primarily focusing on major U.S. exchanges (NYSE and NASDAQ), including all stocks in the S&P 500 index. We're continuously expanding our coverage to include more international markets and smaller-cap stocks.

Can I use Stock Oracle for investment decisions?

Stock Oracle provides predictions and insights that can be valuable for informing investment decisions, but we always recommend using our tool as part of a comprehensive investment strategy. No prediction model can guarantee future results, and all investment decisions should be made with careful consideration of your financial situation and risk tolerance.

How does sentiment analysis work?

Our sentiment analysis system processes thousands of tweets, news articles, and financial reports daily. Using advanced natural language processing techniques, we evaluate the sentiment (positive, neutral, or negative) expressed about specific stocks and the broader market. This sentiment data is then integrated with our technical models to provide a more comprehensive prediction.
